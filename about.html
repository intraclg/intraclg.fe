ABOUT OUR COLLEGE

Pallavan College of Engineering is committed to excellence in education, innovation, and holistic development. Our goal is to produce industry-ready engineers with a global perspective.

OUR JOURNEY

2005

Founded with the mission of empowering students through innovative education. The first batch of students enrolled in undergraduate programs.

2010

Expansion of infrastructure, with state-of-the-art laboratories, hostels, and a new library that supports over 10,000 books and journals.

2015

Collaborated with global universities for student exchange programs, and introduced new cutting-edge courses in AI and Robotics.

2020

Launched online learning platforms and adopted modern technology tools in teaching, ensuring quality education even during the pandemic.

2025

Currently a top engineering college with multiple national and international rankings. Offering 15+ undergraduate and postgraduate programs.
